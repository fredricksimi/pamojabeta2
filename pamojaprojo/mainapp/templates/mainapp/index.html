WELCOME TO PAMOJA ORGANIZATION


Pamoja CBO is a human centered grassroot organization that exists to improve the quality of life of communities in Western Kenya. We are passionate about helping communities develop the skills they need to make positive changes for themselves. We do so by developing, designing, supporting and cascading service based initiatives and programs to people in society who need them the most.

We prioritize health care, education, water & sanitation, youth and women economic empowerment. We have embedded real time approaches to engaging vulnerable children and adolescent women and young girls with the aim of creating happy, able and healthy communities.
